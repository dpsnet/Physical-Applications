# Physical Applications of Unified Field Theory

This repository contains the physical applications and experimental verifications of the Unified Field Theory, focusing on the P series of theoretical modules.

## Repository Structure

### Physical Applications
- **P-1**: Unified Field Theory of Measure Fields, Multiple Twists, and Gauge Theory
- **P-2**: Dirac Operator and Renormalization Group
- **P-3**: Multiple Fractals and String Theory
- **P-4**: Holographic Duality and Quantization
- **P-5**: Dark Matter Theory
- **P-6**: Spacetime Hardening Effect Theory
- **P-7**: Microscopic Time Reversal Experiments and Time Unidirectionality
- **P-8**: Black Hole Theory
- **P-9**: Nuclear Fusion Theory
- **P-10**: Compatible Unification of Loop Quantum Theory and Fractal Clifford Algebra
- **P-11**: Cosmic Origin in Fixed Topology-Dynamic Spectral Dimension Paradigm
- **P-12**: Geometric Interpretation of Fundamental Quantum Mechanical Phenomena
- **P-13**: Multiple Torsion Theory Explanation of Cosmic Filament Spin
- **P-14**: Cosmic Evolution After Black Hole Remnants and High-Dimensional Universe Theory
- **P-15**: Multiverse Physical Laws and Cross-Universe Interaction Theory
- **P-16**: Photon Frequency and Coarse-Grained Quantization Theory
- **P-17**: Geometric Essence of Orthogonal Torsion
- **P-18**: Torsion Evolution Dynamics and Spectral Dimension Flow
- **P-19**: Orthogonal Torsion Theory and Topological Protection Mechanism
- **P-20**: Geometric Unified Theory of Redshift
- **P-21**: Maxwell-Type Equations for Strong Interactions
- **P-22**: Multiple Torsion Calculation Theory for Viscous Fluid Flow
