# Physical Applications of Unified Field Theory

This repository contains the physical applications and experimental verifications of the Unified Field Theory, focusing on the P series of theoretical modules.

- **P-2**: Dirac Operator and Renormalization Group

- **P-5**: Dark Matter Theory
- **P-6**: Spacetime Hardening Effect Theory
- **P-7**: Microscopic Time Reversal Experiments and Time Unidirectionality
- **P-8**: Black Hole Theory
- **P-9**: Nuclear Fusion Theory
- **P-10**: Loop Quantum Theory and Fractal Clifford Algebra Compatibility
- **P-11**: Cosmological Origin in Fixed Topology-Dynamic Spectral Dimension Paradigm
- **P-12**: Geometric Interpretation of Basic Quantum Mechanical Phenomena
- **P-13**: Multiple Twist Theory Explanation of Cosmic Fiber Spin
- **P-14**: Post-Black Hole Remnant Cosmological Evolution and High-Dimensional Universe Theory
- **P-15**: Multiverse Physical Laws and Cross-Universe Interaction Theory
- **P-16**: Photon Frequency and Coarse-Grained Quantization Theory
- **P-17**: Geometric Nature of Orthogonal Twists
- **P-18**: Twist Evolution Dynamics and Spectral Dimension Flow
- **P-19**: Orthogonal Twist Theory and Topological Protection Mechanism
- **P-20**: Geometric Unified Theory of Redshift
- **P-21**: Maxwell-Type Equations for Strong Interactions
